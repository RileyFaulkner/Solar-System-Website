Planets of Our Solar System

Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.

Venus

The hottest planet with a thick, toxic atmosphere.

Earth

Our home planet, the only one known to support life.

Mars

The Red Planet, known for its iron-rich soil.

Jupiter

The largest planet with a giant storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.

Uranus

An ice giant that rotates on its side.

Neptune

The farthest planet, known for its deep blue color.

Overview

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is known for its thick toxic atmosphere and extreme heat.

Facts

  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Moons: 0
  • Orbital period: 225 Earth days
  • Atmosphere: Carbon dioxide and clouds of sulfuric acid

Interesting Feature

Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most planets, meaning the Sun rises in the west.

Overview

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun, known as the Red Planet because of its reddish soil.

Facts

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)
  • Orbital period: 687 Earth days
  • Atmosphere: Carbon dioxide, nitrogen, argon

Interesting Feature

Mars has the tallest volcano in the Solar System, Olympus Mons, standing at 21 km high.

Overview

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun, famous for its spectacular rings made of ice and rock.

Facts

  • Diameter: 120,536 km
  • Moons: 83+
  • Orbital period: 29.46 Earth years
  • Atmosphere: Hydrogen and Helium

Interesting Feature

Saturn's rings are wide but very thin, measuring up to 282,000 km across but less than 1 km thick.

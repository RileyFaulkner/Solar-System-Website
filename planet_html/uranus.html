Overview

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and rotates on its side, giving it extreme seasons.

Facts

  • Diameter: 51,118 km
  • Moons: 27
  • Orbital period: 84 Earth years
  • Atmosphere: Hydrogen, Helium, Methane

Interesting Feature

Uranus’s rotation axis is tilted over 98°, making it appear to roll along its orbit.
